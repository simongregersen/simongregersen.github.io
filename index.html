Simon Oddershede Gregersen

Simon Oddershede Gregersen

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Logic and Semantics group at Aarhus University. I earned my PhD from Aarhus University in March 2023 under the supervision of Lars Birkedal.

I study programming languages and program verification through the lens of higher-order separation logic.
