Simon Oddershede Gregersen

Simon Oddershede Gregersen

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Logic and Semantics group at Aarhus University. I obtained my Ph.D. from Aarhus University, where I was advised by Amin Timany and Lars Birkedal.

I study programming languages and program verification through the lens of higher-order separation logic, particularly for distributed and randomized programs.
